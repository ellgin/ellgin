Selected Case Study

Dental Clinic Dashboard

2022
Internal Tool CRM Product Web
Task

Make it so doctors can see the "live queue" faster, and nurses know when to clean the room.

Action
  • Redesigned schedule board: Separated entries into "Arrived" (White) and "Planned" (Gray).
  • Visual Signals: Added a bold red signal if a patient waits >20 mins.
  • Cabinet Status: Designed room status as the primary signal for cleaning.
  • UX: Kept familiar icons to reduce retraining time.
Result

Approved and handed off to development.

Proposed Success Metrics:
Critical Delays Goal: Reduce % of critical wait times (>20m).
Turnaround Time Accelerate room preparation time.
